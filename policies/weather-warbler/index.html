Weather Warbler Android Application

The Weather Warbler Android application was designed after the Yellow-Rumped Warbler bird.

The application aims to provide a clean, user-friendly weather experience. Modern applications track data, show intrusive ads, and are typically ugly. We don't show ads, don't track you, and nor do we collect any data. You can read more on the privacy policy. The application is currently free and may cost in the future to keep ads away while providing a positive experience. While we try to utilize FOSS technologies, some services will generally cost something to maintain. We'll avoid those services until there is a need or enough demand, which then we'll charge a small fee for those who want that feature.

Please also note, the application is in BETA currently while it is actively being developed. You can expect breaking changes, bugs, and UI changes.

You can find the application for download on the Google Play Store.
